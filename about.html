About TulsaLocal

TulsaLocal is a free local business directory for Tulsa, Oklahoma and the surrounding communities — Broken Arrow, Jenks, Bixby, Owasso, Sand Springs, and Sapulpa.

Every listing is verified, local, and completely free to claim. No credit card, no hidden fees, ever. Our goal is to make it easy for Tulsa residents to discover and support the businesses that make this community home.

Who We Are

TulsaLocal is managed by WebbDesignz, a Tulsa-based digital marketing and local SEO company. We built this directory because we believe local businesses deserve visibility without paying for it.
