About Owasso, Oklahoma

One of Oklahoma's fastest-growing cities, located north of Tulsa with excellent schools and a booming retail corridor. Part of the Tulsa Metropolitan Statistical Area, Owasso offers residents easy access to Tulsa's amenities while maintaining its own distinct community identity.

Local businesses in Owasso serve both residents and the broader Tulsa metro area. From dining and retail to home services and healthcare, the Owasso business community is a vital part of Green Country Oklahoma.
