About Bixby, Oklahoma

The Green Country community known for agriculture, rapid residential growth, and highly rated schools south of Tulsa. Part of the Tulsa Metropolitan Statistical Area, Bixby offers residents easy access to Tulsa's amenities while maintaining its own distinct community identity.

Local businesses in Bixby serve both residents and the broader Tulsa metro area. From dining and retail to home services and healthcare, the Bixby business community is a vital part of Green Country Oklahoma.
