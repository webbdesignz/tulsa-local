About Sapulpa, Oklahoma

Historic Route 66 community southwest of Tulsa, home to the famous Frankoma Pottery and a strong local business tradition. Part of the Tulsa Metropolitan Statistical Area, Sapulpa offers residents easy access to Tulsa's amenities while maintaining its own distinct community identity.

Local businesses in Sapulpa serve both residents and the broader Tulsa metro area. From dining and retail to home services and healthcare, the Sapulpa business community is a vital part of Green Country Oklahoma.
