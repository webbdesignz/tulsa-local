About Sand Springs, Oklahoma

An industrial and residential city west of Tulsa along the Arkansas River, known for its parks and community character. Part of the Tulsa Metropolitan Statistical Area, Sand Springs offers residents easy access to Tulsa's amenities while maintaining its own distinct community identity.

Local businesses in Sand Springs serve both residents and the broader Tulsa metro area. From dining and retail to home services and healthcare, the Sand Springs business community is a vital part of Green Country Oklahoma.
