About Jenks, Oklahoma

The Antique Capital of Oklahoma, located along the Arkansas River with the popular Riverwalk Crossing entertainment district. Part of the Tulsa Metropolitan Statistical Area, Jenks offers residents easy access to Tulsa's amenities while maintaining its own distinct community identity.

Local businesses in Jenks serve both residents and the broader Tulsa metro area. From dining and retail to home services and healthcare, the Jenks business community is a vital part of Green Country Oklahoma.
